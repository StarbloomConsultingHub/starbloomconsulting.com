# LinkedIn Post 1 — "From Winner to Judge"
## Publish: Week 1 | Tone: Confident, earned authority

---

In 2023, I entered the Maryland Home Growers Cup. Won it. Came back in 2024 and won again — 1st Place Judges' Choice with Trop Cherry. Then in 2025, Fingerboard Farm called and asked me to sit on the judge panel.

That's the credential nobody talks about.

Not a certification. Not a course completion badge. It's the moment your peers — the people you competed against — look at your work and say "we want him deciding who wins next."

I've spent my career in industries where the only credential that matters is whether you can deliver. Construction. Erosion control. Behavioral intervention. Large-scale production facilities with 50-person teams and 21-room rotational schedules. Nobody handed me a title. I built the systems, wrote the SOPs from scratch, and the results spoke.

That's the same standard I apply to Starbloom Consulting. I don't build AI automation for industries I haven't worked in. I don't sell platforms I haven't stress-tested on my own hardware. Edge AI orchestration, 24/7 intake engines, legacy data bridges — all built by someone who's run real crews on real deadlines with real consequences.

The tools are different. The mindset isn't.
